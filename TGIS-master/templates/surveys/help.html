Help & Documentation

How to Use

  • Upload your CSV file using the Upload CSV page. Only .csv files up to 5MB are accepted.
  • Map your CSV columns to required fields (Kitta Number, Latitude, Longitude, Owner Name).
  • Preview your data on the interactive map and table before confirming.
  • Download KML or error reports as needed.
  • View your upload history and manage files from the dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What file types are supported? Only .csv files with valid structure and content.
  • How are errors reported? Errors are shown after upload and can be downloaded as a CSV report.
  • How do I see my previous uploads? Go to the dashboard to view your upload history and download files.
  • Is my data secure? All uploads are scanned for dangerous content and only authenticated users can access their data.
  • How do I get help? Contact support or refer to this help page for guidance.

Tips for Large Files

  • Split very large CSVs into smaller chunks if you encounter performance issues.
  • Ensure your CSV has clear headers and no extra formatting.
  • Contact support for bulk upload options.

Mobile & Accessibility

  • The application is fully responsive and works on all modern devices.
  • Use the navigation menu to access all features on mobile.
